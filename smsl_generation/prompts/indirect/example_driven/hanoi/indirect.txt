## Puzzle

3 stands: `a`=brown, `b`=blue, `c`=red. 3 disks: 1=gray(small), 2=yellow(med), 3=green(large).
- Move one disk per operation to a different stand.
- Only the top disk on a stand can move.
- A larger disk cannot be placed on a smaller disk.

**Top disk definition:** Disk `d` is on top of peg `p` only if no smaller-numbered disk is also on `p`.
