# Concise River Crossing Direct Prompt

Generate the complete SMSL JSON for the river crossing puzzle.

4 entities: grass, sheep, steve, wolf. 3 locations: 0=red_land, B=boat, 1=green_land.

Rules: adjacent moves only (0↔B, B↔1). Sheep+wolf or sheep+grass at same non-boat location without steve is invalid. Boat holds ≤2; if 2, one must be steve.

States: `State_<grass><sheep><steve><wolf>`. 40 valid. Initial: `State_0000`.
Operations: `move_<entity>_from_<location>_to_<location>`. Names: 0→red_land, B→boat, 1→green_land.

Under each state, list all valid operations with their resulting states.
